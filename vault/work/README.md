# Work

Your private project and engagement notes, plus your work knowledge wiki. Private by default.

> **Engine:** all wiki operations here follow `../AGENTS.md`.

## Structure

- `wiki/` — work knowledge wiki
  - `wiki/raw/{inbox,meetings,notes,media,_archive}/` — sources (manual drops, meeting notes,
    quick/voice notes, uploaded media, archive)
  - `wiki/pages/{sources,entities,concepts,synthesis,references}/` — synthesized pages
- `initiatives/` — your per-project / per-engagement tracking (PM). Track work here, not in ad-hoc folders.
- `deliverables/` (+ `_templates/`) — formal documents you author (drafts).
- `records/` — your official / signed copies.
- `artifacts/` — diagrams, decks, exports.
- `daily/` — daily notes (see `daily/_templates/daily.md`). Today's note is created by the
  Daily Notes plugin.

## What goes here

- Project and engagement notes
- Meeting summaries and call notes
- Observations, strategies, and decisions (unfiltered, for your eyes)
- Anything you want a private work brain to remember

## What does NOT go here

- Personal cognitive content → use `second-brain/`
- Code repositories → use the root `projects/` folder
- Throwaway scratch → use the root `scratch/` folder
