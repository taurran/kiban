# Professional
Your professional life, growth, development, and goals — employer-independent and permanent.
The career is yours; the job is a tenant (that lives in `work/`, only if you use this vault for work).

- `goals/` — career goals and long arcs
- `areas/` — ongoing domains of practice and responsibility
- `people/` — professional network; `people/mentorship/` for mentor/mentee pages and session notes
- `events/` — career calendar: conferences, reviews, cert renewals, professional travel
- `growth/` — skills inventory, learning, certifications
- `portfolio/` — wins, shipped work, brag doc; `portfolio/brand/` for resume, public bio, talks
- `insights/` — synthesis landing zone (machine-written)
